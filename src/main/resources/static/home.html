About ESVP

ESVP (Explorer, Shopper, Vacationer, Prisoner) is a retrospective technique used to gauge participants' attitudes toward a meeting or workshop.

Participants anonymously identify themselves as one of the following:

  • Explorer - Excited to learn and discover new things
  • Shopper - Looking for specific value to take away
  • Vacationer - Happy to be away from regular work
  • Prisoner - Forced to attend, would rather be elsewhere

This tool helps facilitators understand the mindset of participants and adjust accordingly.

Try Mood Swing

Mood Swing allows participants to rate their mood on a scale from 1 to 10, where:

  • 1 - Not good at all
  • 10 - Impressed

This tool helps facilitators understand the overall mood of participants during a session.
